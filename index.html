🔒 Privacy Policy - JMS PIN_AGENT

Effective Date: May 01, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how JMS PIN_AGENT ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the AutoPin Pro Agent service.

1. Information We Collect

AutoPin Pro Agent only accesses your Pinterest account through the official Pinterest API to:

  • Create and publish pins on your behalf
  • Read your board list for pin placement
  • Store OAuth authentication tokens locally on your server

We do not collect: passwords, personal messages, follower lists, or any data beyond what the Pinterest API requires for pin creation.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your Pinterest API tokens are used solely to authenticate requests to Pinterest's API and create pins with your affiliate product content.

3. Data Storage & Security

All authentication tokens and configuration data are stored locally in an encrypted SQLite database (autopin.db) on your AWS EC2 instance. You have full control over this server and its data.

We do not transmit, share, or sell your data to any third party.

4. Your Rights & Controls

  • 🔐 Revoke Access: Disconnect the app anytime via Pinterest Settings → Apps
  • 🗑️ Delete Data: Remove autopin.db from your server to delete all stored tokens
  • 📧 Contact Us: Email us to request data clarification or deletion
